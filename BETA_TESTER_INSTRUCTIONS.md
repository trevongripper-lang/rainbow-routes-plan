# Tribe Trips — Beta Tester Instructions

Thanks for helping shape Tribe Trips! This doc is your starting kit.

## Before you start

- You'll create an account at the preview URL we sent you.
- On first sign-in you'll see a short **Beta consent** screen — please read it
  and check the boxes that apply to you.
- If anything in the consent screen doesn't sit right with you, just close the
  tab and email `hello@tgklabs.io` — no pressure.

## Required consent (also captured in-app at `/beta-consent`)

Please tick each before testing:

- [ ] I am 18 years or older.
- [ ] I understand Tribe Trips is in private beta and may contain bugs or
      incomplete features.
- [ ] I consent to Tribe Trips reviewing my screen recording, voice narration,
      written feedback, and usage notes for product improvement.
- [ ] I understand I should not enter payment card details, passport
      information, private addresses, real confirmation numbers, health
      information, or other sensitive information while testing.
- [ ] I understand I can stop testing or recording at any time.
- [ ] I understand my beta feedback and recordings may be stored for up to
      6 months and then deleted.

## What we'd love you to try

1. Sign up and complete the consent screen.
2. Create a trip, then **invite someone by copying the invite link and
   sending it to them manually** (text, email, WhatsApp, group chat —
   whatever you'd normally use). Tribe Trips does not email trip invites
   automatically during beta. Play with destinations / votes / chat.
3. Add a flight, a stay, a couple of costs, then settle up.
4. Install Tribe Trips to your phone's home screen (see below) and repeat the
   same flow as a PWA.

## Installing on iPhone (Safari only)

iOS Safari doesn't show a native install prompt, so the steps are manual:

1. Open Tribe Trips in **Safari** (not Chrome or Firefox).
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with an up arrow) in Safari's bottom bar.
3. Scroll the share sheet and choose **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Tap **Add**. The Tribe Trips icon will appear on your home screen and
   launch full-screen like a native app.

On Android Chrome / Edge / desktop Chromium, use the **Install** button in
the app — it triggers the native install prompt.

## Sending feedback

- **Quick reactions** — just message the founder in whichever channel you were
  invited from. One-line "this felt weird" is gold.
- **Screen recordings** — iOS Control Center → screen record, or QuickTime on
  Mac. Talk over it if you can. Send the file to `hello@tgklabs.io`.
- **Written feedback** — same email, no template required.

## Sensitive information — please don't

When recording or sharing screenshots:

- Use **placeholder names, fake confirmation numbers, scrambled email addresses**.
- Don't enter your **real payment card** — payments are sandbox during beta,
  so test cards are the right choice.
- Skip **passport / ID images**, **private home addresses**, **medical info**,
  or anything you wouldn't want a small product team to see.

## Stopping or deleting

- You can stop testing at any time. No follow-up required.
- You can delete your account from **Settings → My account → Delete account**.
- To delete recordings, voice notes, or written feedback you sent us, email
  `hello@tgklabs.io` and we'll wipe them.

## Retention

Beta feedback and recordings are kept for up to **6 months**, then deleted —
unless we need to keep something specific for security, legal, or product
integrity reasons.

## Questions

Anything unclear — `hello@tgklabs.io`. Thanks again for testing!
